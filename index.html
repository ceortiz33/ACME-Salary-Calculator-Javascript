ACME Salary Calculator

Instructions:

  1. Create a txt file.
  2. Add elements with the format Employee=DayHH:MM-HH:MM,DayHH:MM-HH:MM,DayHH:MM-HH:MM then Enter.
  3. Click in "Choose file", pick the txt file you create and wait for the results.

Notes:

  • Range of hours must be between these time periods: 00:01-09:00, 09:01-18:00, 18:01-00:00
  • You can add the same day in other salary range RENE=MO10:00-12:00,MO15:00-17:00 .
  • Day must be MO,TU,WE,TH,FR,SA or SU representing Monday,Tuesday,Wednesday,Thursday,Friday,Saturdary and Sunday respectively.
  • The order doesn't matter however is recommended to write the days of work starting from Monday and ending in Sunday.
  • Hours must be between 0 and 23, Minutes must be between 0 and 59.
